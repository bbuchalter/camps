Welcome to DevCamps.org!

DevCamps are a system for easily managing development, integration, staging, and production environments. Each environment is called a camp.

Focused on web applications, camps make developers more efficient, and make the agile development and deployment lifecycle easy:

  • Business users and developers easily collaborate on projects in real time
  • Quickly get new developers up to speed without hours of setup on their own computers
  • Works with existing version control and continuous integration systems
  • Easily juggle multiple simultaneous projects
  • Provide superior coordination for teams of developers
  • Keep development databases in sync with production

DevCamps were developed at End Point and are in extensive use on several dozen sites.
